Swedish Museum of Natural History

Register today

Symposium With ambition to bridge a gap

The symposium has the ambition to bridge the gap between Public Management Officials, Systems Developers, and User/Research Communities in regard to Open Source Systems availability for management practices and research.

Schedule

This is a one day symposium arranged by the Bioninformatics and genetics department at the Swedish museum of Natural History (NRM/BIO).
